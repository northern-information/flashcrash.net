Flash Crash 2021 — 2022

Founded in the throes of the pandemic in 2021, Flash Crash began as a Teletype-only performance. It was suggested that it become a "recursive" event in which performers for the next event would sign up as part of the finale of the current event. "To be changed by the world..." was the original call to arms. Soon after we added a mission statement. We have extensive archives of most performances. The finale show was on August 6, 2022.
