Flash Crash 2021 — 2022

Founded in the throes of the pandemic in 2021, Flash Crash began as a Teletype-only performance showcase. Towards the end of the stream, it was suggested to become a "recursive" event: signups for next time open on stream as the show closes. "To be changed by the world..." was our original call to arms. Soon after we added a mission statement. We have extensive archives of most performances. The final show was on August 6, 2022.
